About Us

üèõÔ∏è History of the Department of Information Technology

The Department of Information Technology at Government Engineering College Idukki (GECI) was established in 2000 to meet the growing demand for professionals in the field of computing and information sciences. Since its inception, the department has been dedicated to providing quality technical education and fostering innovation among students. Starting with a modest intake of students, the department has gradually expanded its academic programs, laboratories, and research facilities. Over the years, it has kept pace with the rapid advancements in the IT industry by introducing updated curricula, modern laboratories, and hands-on learning experiences. The department has consistently focused on developing both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, equipping graduates to excel in diverse domains such as software engineering, networking, data science, cybersecurity, and artificial intelligence.

üéì Accreditation & Recognition

The Department of Information Technology, GECI, is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, ensuring quality and standards in technical education. The programs are affiliated to the APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU), Kerala, following its academic regulations and curriculum framework. GECI is a Government institution under the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Kerala, known for imparting affordable and quality engineering education. The department actively participates in NBA accreditation processes to maintain and enhance academic quality, infrastructure, and teaching standards. Recognition through student achievements, alumni success, research contributions, and industry collaborations further strengthens the reputation of the department.

Admissions

The Information Technology department at Government Engineering College Idukki (GECI) offers B.Tech admissions as per the guidelines of APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) and the Government of Kerala. Students can apply through various entry routes including merit-based, management quota, and lateral entry programs.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as core subjects.
  • Minimum aggregate marks as per KTU/Govt. guidelines.
  • Lateral entry: Diploma holders in relevant engineering streams.

Admission Process

  • Apply through the KEAM (Kerala Engineering Architecture Medical) portal for merit-based admission.
  • Management quota admissions are handled by the college through a separate application process.
  • Lateral entry admissions for diploma holders are conducted as per KTU guidelines.
  • Shortlisted candidates must attend counseling and document verification.

Important Documents

  • 10th and 12th Mark Sheets / Diploma Certificate
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) and Conduct Certificate
  • Migration Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • ID Proof (Aadhaar, Passport, or other government ID)
